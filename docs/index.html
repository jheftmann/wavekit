WaveKit

Surf forecasts in your macOS menu bar

Description

WaveKit gives you quick access to surf conditions for your favorite spots. It shows wave heights, surf ratings, wind, and tides — all from your menu bar.

  • 16-day extended forecast with daily ratings
  • Today's conditions with swell, wind, and tide data
  • Sorts spots by distance from your location
  • Click any spot to open it on Surfline

Download

Download WaveKitv1.0.0

Requires macOS 14.0 (Sonoma) or later

Installation

  1. Download and unzip WaveKit.zip
  2. Drag WaveKit.app to your Applications folder
  3. Open WaveKit from Applications
  4. If you see a security warning, right-click the app and select "Open"

Usage

Adding spots: Click the + button, then paste a Surfline URL (e.g. from surfline.com/surf-report/...).

Forecast view: Shows a 16-day outlook. Scroll horizontally to see more days.

Today view: Shows detailed conditions for today including swell, wind, and tides.

Tip: Sign in with your Surfline account in Settings to unlock the full 16-day forecast.
